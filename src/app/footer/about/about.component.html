The team behind Calendar.ly

We are a very small team. Calendar.ly is a really young app. We've been working on it for a year and we are almost ready to launch our Closed Beta.

What we are

We put together Content Calendar and project management in the cloud. Both tools will give you the structure to improve the creative and approval processes that lead to great content creation.

Create

Brief your team on a specific content piece for your next campaign.

Provive feedback on the spot

Stakeholders have access to the latest feedbacks at the same time as your team. Everyone is on the same page.

Get ready for publishing

Approve your social and blog posts in time! Never lose the big picture.

What we are not

Research & Social Listening

We are not a social listening tool to help you research on buzz around a specific topic so you can understand what your audience point of view is.

Briefing & Calendar.ly

This is where Calendar.ly helps you! No more content calendar templates that get outdated. Brief your team using Calendar.ly and stay always up-to-date birdseye view of your content creation efforts so you can publish on time.

Publishing & Monitoring

Post your approved content into your social media profiles using your prefered scheduling tools or directly into each social profile.
